About
A strategist who builds,
not just advises

Giovanni Gavino Everduin — most people call him Gio — is a Harvard Business School alum (GMP21) with over two decades of experience driving transformation across banking, fintech, and digital innovation. A true citizen of the world, he has lived and worked across 10 countries spanning Europe, North America, Central America and the Middle East.

Currently the Chief Strategy & Innovation Officer and Head of Ventures at Commercial Bank International (CBI) in Dubai, and Co-founder of CBIx — CBI's independent innovation subsidiary exploring AI, tokenized assets, Web3, gaming, and next-gen banking models.

Before CBI, Gio held senior roles at Accenture London (advising Fortune 500 boards), Emirates NBD's Tanfeeth (as Chief People Officer), and ran his own consulting firm, ETNICITY Group. He speaks at global stages including Token2049, GITEX, Fintech Surge, and COP28, and mentors early-stage founders through accelerators like Ascend.

The person behind
the persona

By day, Gio runs strategy and innovation for a bank. By night and over weekends, he exists across multiple identities in fundamentally different communities — from Bitcoin Ordinals maxis who'd lose it if they knew he works at a bank, to Ethereum collectors, Solana degens, and hip-hop heads.

His NFT collection includes 1/1 art pieces by established artists like Tony Tafuro, Burnt Toast, and Chainszy — each representing a different facet of who he is across the real world, Discord, Telegram, and various SoFi platforms. His alter egos span an OMB, a Doodle, a ZMB, and a Bearmarketeer.

He also produces films and documentaries, has provided creative direction for fashion brands, and once produced an award-winning documentary on women's empowerment called "SHE". Outside of screens, you'll find him reading The Business of Fashion, going down HBR rabbit holes at 5AM, or collecting art that tells stories worth telling.

What It Really Takes for Banks to Innovate Without Breaking Trust
Couchonomics with Arjun · Dec 2025
Gio joins the couch to unpack what innovation really looks like inside a regulated bank — from legacy systems and institutional mindsets to AI, digital assets, and on-chain finance. A candid conversation that goes beyond buzzwords to explore where real value is being created. He shares why regulation can act as a catalyst for innovation, how banks and fintechs are moving from competition to partnership, and why the future of finance will be hybrid rather than fully on-chain or off-chain. Covers CBIX, tokenization, stablecoins, privacy, and why understanding the problem always matters more than chasing the latest technology.

Tajikistan Proposes AI Regulation Initiative at the United Nations
Asia-Plus · Sep 2024
Tajikistan introduced a resolution at the United Nations to establish international regulatory mechanisms for AI that balance technological progress with the interests of developing nations. The initiative — which the UN General Assembly unanimously adopted in July 2025 — proposes a unified AI self-regulation space and joint research-sharing across countries. It includes the creation of a Regional AI Center in Dushanbe. Gio has served as an advisor to Tajikistan's Ministry of Industry & New Technologies, contributing to the country's broader AI and innovation strategy.
