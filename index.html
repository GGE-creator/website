About

A strategist who builds,
not just advises

Giovanni Gavino Everduin — most people call him Gio — is a Harvard Business School alum (GMP21) with over two decades of experience driving transformation across banking, fintech, and digital innovation. A true citizen of the world, he has lived and worked across 10 countries spanning Europe, North America, Central America and the Middle East.

Currently the Chief Strategy & Innovation Officer and Head of Ventures at Commercial Bank International (CBI) in Dubai, and Co-founder of CBIx — CBI's independent innovation subsidiary exploring AI, tokenized assets, Web3, gaming, and next-gen banking models.

Before CBI, Gio held senior roles at Accenture in London (advising Fortune 500 firms), Emirates NBD's Tanfeeth (as Chief People Officer), and ran his own consulting firm. He speaks at global stages including Ai4 Las Vegas, the UN, GITEX, InMerge and GFTN, and mentors early-stage founders through accelerators like Ascend - the world's first ever RWA accelerator - and the SUI Foundation.

The person behind
the persona

By day, Gio runs strategy and innovation for a bank. By night and over weekends, he exists across multiple identities in fundamentally different communities — from Bitcoin Ordinals maxis who'd lose it if they knew he works at a bank, to Ethereum collectors, Solana degens, and hip-hop heads.

His on-chain art collection includes 1/1 art pieces by artists like Tony Tafuro, No Legs, Scrog and Rupture — each representing a different facet of who he is across the real world, Discord, Telegram, and various SoFi platforms. His alter egos span an OMB, a Doodle, a ZMB, and a Bearmarketeer.

He also produces films and documentaries, has provided creative direction for fashion brands, and once produced an award-winning documentary on women's empowerment called "SHE". Outside of screens, you'll find him reading The Business of Fashion, going down HBR rabbit holes at 5AM, or collecting art that tells stories worth telling.
